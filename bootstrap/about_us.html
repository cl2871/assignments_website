About Us


Who We Are

We are a venture capital firm located in Menlo Park, California that specializes in providing funding to various local small businesses as well as large tech startups within the Silicon Valley. While our main focus is within the Bay Area, we occasionally invest in several select startup companies internationally. Aside from providing venture capital services and small business lending, we also invest in public companies to diversify our company portfolio.

Our Values

At Sunflower Capital, we place great emphasis on teamwork and highly value the clients we work with. Not only do we invest heavily into the businesses we choose to become successful, but we also devote a lot of our resources towards picking the best people to lead and make the best decisions for us and our affiliates. We believe in our entrepreneurs, in their dreams, and in their capacity to strive to achieve success in their respective industries. We hold financial sustainability as our main priority, so we connect with founders and leaders that aim for long-term success and understand when to take on necessary risk and when to move on. We take losses and mistakes as lessons for the future, and we strive to not only move on, but also move forward.

Sunflower

Our founder has always been fond of the beautiful sunflowers that grew near his hometown in southern California, so he dedicated this company to those beautiful flowers that became iconic of his childhood memories. He hoped that his company would provide the right conditions so that others could sprout up and grow to become amazing like those majestic perennials. Additionally, Sunflower Capital supports environmental policies and green initiatives, so we invest in green technology and encourage our companies and businesses to make environmentally-conscious decisions.
