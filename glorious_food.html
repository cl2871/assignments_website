Glorious Food

Hello! Welcome to my "Glorious Food" page, a page dedicated towards glorifying food. As a representative of the human race, I can confidently say that everyone, or at least the vast majority of people, enjoys food. Food is a wonderful substance of varying forms that not only provides us nourishment, but also provides us positive emotions. Through regional availability and the culinary arts, food has influenced and characterized the cultures of all these unqiue nations around the world. Furthermore, through our experiences with eating, we are able to develop relationships with others as well as construct our attitudes towards many aspects of society and our lives.

Favorite Foods

Some of my favorite foods include pasta bolognese, Japanese curry, smoked salmon, Korean BBQ beef short ribs, Louisiana crab cakes, and BBQ pork buns. While I enjoy a variety of different kinds of food, a simple dish I have always enjoyed throughout my childhood is tomato and egg rice. While the two main variants are savory and sweet, both of which are delicious, my favorite is the sweet kind, the variant that uses ketchup. Below is a simple recipe for this dish.

Stir-Fried Tomato and Egg Rice

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  • white rice

Preparation

  1. First beat eggs with 1/2 teaspoon salt in a bowl. Then partially cook the eggs with oil in a skillet. Afterwards, set the eggs aside.
  2. Replace the eggs with chopped tomatoes, stir-frying them in the skillet at medium to high heat. Add in ketchup, sugar, and salt as the tomatoes get cooked.
  3. Add the eggs back in and cook everything thoroughly.
  4. Pour the tomato and egg mixture over rice and serve.
